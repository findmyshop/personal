Program Issues

If the program freezes, exit the program and close all other browser windows on your machine. Clear the cache on your computer by opening a new browser window and clicking on “settings”, “history” and “clear cache” and then logging back on to the program. Keep other browsers and browser windows closed while you are in the program to minimize the chance of future problems with freezing. Slow internet connections can cause the video to load slowly and the system may appear to stop or the audio and video may be out of sync.
