Options Review Segments

When you are in the Options Review segment, the videos will advance automatically until the end of the segment. If you want to hear any of the videos again, click on “previous message”. To replay the current video, click on “Replay message.” To advance to the next video yourself, click on “Next message.” You can also click on the buttons to the right of the video for more information on those topics. Once you review a second video, you can click on “Next message” to return to where you were in the Options Review Segment.
